01 / zero clutter

Lives in your System Tray

KeyHolder remains completely out of the way, operating silently next to your clock. With a click or a simple global shortcut, the clean overlay slides open instantly, ready to deliver credentials.

02 / ironclad security

Hardware-Backed Vaults

We separate metadata from secrets. Your platform names and labels are stored locally in light metadata files, but all secrets are written directly to macOS Keychain or Windows Credential Locker. Absolutely zero cleartext secrets are ever written to disk.

03 / zero lag

Touch ID & Windows Hello

Credentials are unlocked on-demand. Simply trigger a copy, verify your fingerprint with Touch ID or Windows Hello, and the credential decrypts and copies to your clipboard in under 1ms, re-locking itself immediately.

04 / intelligent mapping

Smart Platform Auto-Mapping

KeyHolder automatically maps your credential names to themed colors and platform icons (AI, Version Control, Hosting, Payment, Databases, Productivity). No manual organization or tagging required.

Security Architecture

Built for developer peace of mind

KeyHolder operates strictly locally. Your secret keys never touch any external server or cleartext storage.

🔒

System Locker Integrations

Uses macOS Keychain Services and Windows Credential Locker APIs. Secrets are encrypted using hardware-backed credentials and only decrypted for a millisecond to load into memory.

Biometric Gate
